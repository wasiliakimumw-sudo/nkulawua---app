How Year-End Rollover Works
  1. Preview: See all clients and their outstanding balances that will be carried forward.
  2. Confirm: After reviewing, execute the rollover to create opening balances.
  3. Result: Each client's outstanding balance becomes their "Opening Balance" (Balance Before) for the new fiscal year.
What happens:
  • Beneficiary's Outstanding Balance → Becomes Opening Balance (B/F)
  • Opening balances are recorded for each active client
  • A rollover record is created for audit trail
